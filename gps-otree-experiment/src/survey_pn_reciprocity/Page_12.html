How would you decide?

Assume that you are abroad and need medical treatment. In the country you are in it is common that the doctor treats patients only with cash. The treatment costs about 100 Euro. You do not have any cash with you. A stranger in the waiting room observes the situation and gives 100 Euro as a gift to you. You are happy to take the gift. You ask the stranger for their address. When returning home two weeks later you decide that you want to thank the stranger and send them a present.
