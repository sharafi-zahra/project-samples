How would you decide?

Imagine the following situation:

Together with a person whom you do not know you won 100 Euro in a lottery. The rules stipulate the following:

One of you has to make a proposal about how to divide the 100 Euro between you two. The other one gets to know the proposal and has to decide between two options. He or she can accept the proposal or reject it. If he or she accepts the proposal, the money is divided according to the proposal. If he or she rejects the proposal, both receive nothing.
